About CSEC

The Computer Science Enrichment Club is a student-run group at the University of Toronto Scarborough which focuses on helping students with a passion for Computer Science take the next step. We do everything from hosting monthly learning seminars to helping you create your own website.

Learn...

Algorithms, Network Security, and Web Development in our weekly meetings led by qualified lecturers

Create...

Amazing applications and lasting friendships through our Hackathons, Contests, and more!

Discover...

Interests you never knew you had through our engaging and exciting monthly seminars on everything from Machine Learning to PC Building

Three divisions, Three times the fun

Interested in something specific? Join one of our three comprehensive subdivisions to delve deep into each subject. Memberships are always free and we meet weekly to host discussions and teach important topics.
