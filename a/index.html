The Computer Science Enrichment Club is a student-run group that focuses on helping students with a passion for Computer Science take the next steps in their career. We focus on teaching the basics of competitive programming, which will help you solve problems on competitions like the ACM-ICPC, Google Codejam, and Facebook Hackercup.
