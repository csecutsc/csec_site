CSEC's Monthly Seminars and other Outstanding Events

Chess Engine Programming

Friday October 20, 6PM-8PM @ IC230

From wowing crowds in 1997 by besting Chess Grandmaster Garry Kasparov to now standing toe to toe with Dota 2 pros, Artificial Intelligence is constantly improving and evolving. Join CSEC in IC230 this Friday October 20th from 6PM-8PM as we take a look at the basics of AI with guest speaker Rhys and explore the intricacies of developing a Chess engine from scratch.
Note: This event has concluded. You can view the video once it is uploaded here

Tech Interview Prep

Friday October 27, 4PM-7PM @ The Hub [IC Bottom Floor]

Internship season is upon us! Join CSEC in The Hub this Friday October 27th as we explain how to ace your coding interview. We'll have mentors with extensive interviewing experience on-hand to help you understand the techniques and strategies of solving a variety of coding questions. Practice mock interviews, analyze solution structures, and land your dream job with some help from our mentors!

Developer Circles Community Challenge

November 20, 2017

Over a dozen prizes worth more than $150,000 will be awarded, including three $5,000 student team awards.

Hack Your Travel with OpenShift

December 5, 2017

To enter Red Hat's OpenShift hackathon, build an app that helps travelers get around town, plan a trip, or find guidance. All entries must be built on or migrated to the OpenShift Online platform and integrate JBoss Middleware. Cash prizes total $30,000, including a $2,000 student team award.

Learn more!
